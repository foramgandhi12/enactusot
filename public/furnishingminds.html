Furnishing Minds

Furnishing minds was developed in response to the shutdowns amidst COVID-19, and has to date provided over 55 families and almost 100 students with free educational resource packages. These packages include picture books, guided reading sets, teaching resources, board games, puzzles, craft materials and other youth educational material.
Enactus OTU will be developing partnerships with key stakeholders to set up the supply and distribution of educational subscription boxes to youth in low income families. Our Furnishing Minds team will work on setting up a website for subscribers to create personalized accounts, and the distribution logistics to get educational content to kids who need it the most.
