What Is Skill Series

Skill Series inspires and educates students in a wide area of topics, that are not taught at school, with the right resources and individuals.

Skill Series is a project that inspires and educates students in a wide range of topics with the right resources. From design to financial literacy, and from public speaking to leadership, Skill Series will encourage students to find new passions and skills that are not taught at school with the help of guest speakers and businesses.
