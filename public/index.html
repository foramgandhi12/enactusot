About Us

What Is Enactus?

Enactus is a community of entrepreneurial leaders who see business as a way to address social issues.

  • Entrepreneurial - igniting business innovation with integrity and passion
  • Action - the experience of social impact that sparks social enterprise
  • Us - student, academic and business leaders collaborating to make a better world

Our vision is to create a better and more sustainable world. We utilize the power of conscious capitalism for positive change, and we are committed to shaping the world with an optimistic, yet competitive spirit. We rally the energy, ideas and passion of the best and brightest students to create and implement empowerment projects and business ventures in our community.
