Enactus OT

Our Mission

Enactus Ontario Tech aims to create a diverse, inclusive, and collaborative environment for its members to develop and harness their entrepreneurial skills through experiential learning, to create and implement socially conscious projects in the Durham community and beyond.

Enactus OT

Core Values

  • Environmentally conscious and sustainable behavior
  • Commitment to empowering youth in entrepreneurship
  • Encouraging innovation and efficiency
  • Striving to develop leadership
  • Creating an accountable, diverse and inclusive team
